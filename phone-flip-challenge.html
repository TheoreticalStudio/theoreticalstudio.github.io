About the game

Do you like spinning things? Like fidget toys, pens, or phones? Try the Phone Flip challenge!

Phone Flip is a fun and simple game where you flip your real phone into the air and try to catch it. Sounds easy? Try it once — and you won’t stop. It’s all about skill, fast hands, and perfect timing. Flip your phone, catch it just right, and don’t drop it!
