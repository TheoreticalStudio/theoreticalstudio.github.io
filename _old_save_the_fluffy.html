About the game

Save the Fluffy is an action-driven puzzle game where you can Save the Fluffy by moving (or just dodging) bullets, pushing dynamite away from Fluffy and closer to bandits, stopping the train in its tracks, and you can even Rewind Time! Your goal is to make sure that nothing bad happens with Fluffy! If anything goes wrong – Rewind Time!
