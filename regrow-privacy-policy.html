Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25.10.2021

Theoretical Studio (“us”, “we”, or “our”) represented by sole proprietor Shishov Gleb Aleksandrovich operates the Regrow Arena mobile game application (the “Game”).

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Game and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the Game. By using the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

What type of data is collected and from what sources?

We use third-party services described below to collect your data while you are using the Game. The information we collect is non-identifiable to you. For example, we will collect device information and device identifiers, as well as events completed or actions taken within the Game (the “Data”):

  • User interface interaction events (number and order of button presses)
  • Time spent in-game

Google Analytics for Firebase (Google Inc.)

Google Analytics for Firebase or Firebase Analytics is an analytics service provided by Google Inc. In order to understand Google's use of Data, consult Google's partner policy Firebase Analytics may share Data with other tools provided by Firebase, such as Crash Reporting, Authentication, Remote Config or Notifications. The User may check this privacy policy to find a detailed explanation about the other tools used by the Owner. We use identifiers for mobile devices (including Android Advertising ID or Advertising Identifier for iOS, respectively) and technologies similar to cookies to run the Firebase Analytics service. Personal Data collected: Usage Data.

When you purchase in-Game content or features using the Google Play Store, we may receive your email address, zip code, city, state or country, and purchase history. You can view their Privacy Policy, which includes instructions on how to restrict the collection of Personal Information.

Who do we share this data with?

We share this data in an anonymous form with Unity Technologies (“Unity”), our service provider for analytics. As such, by using the Game you are also agreeing to Unity’s privacy policy, which can be found here: Unity 3d privacy policy.

When we use third-parties like Unity, we enter into contracts with them where they are obliged to maintain the confidentiality of the information we share with them and use it only for the purpose related to the supply of their services to us.

Why do we collect the Data?

We use the Data to make more informed decisions about the Game and improve your play experience. The Data can help troubleshoot and address any bugs, technical issues or errors. Unity will send us reports on this Data which gives us insight on the Game, how players use it, how the Game can be improved and how we can support updates.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
