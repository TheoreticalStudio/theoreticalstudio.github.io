How to delete account

>

In order to delete your account as well as your personal information you need to:

  1. Send us an email with a Delete Account topic
  2. Attach to your email a screenshot of your Main Menu screen in Regrow so that your id is visible!

That's all. After receiving your email, we will immediately delete your account as well as your personal information that we have
