How to Delete account

In order to delete your account as well as your personal information you need to:

  1. Go to settings(gear icon in main menu on top left angle)
  2. Take a screenshot settings screen
  3. Send us an email with a Delete Account topic
  4. Attach to your email a screenshot of your Settings screen in Regrow so that your id is visible!

That's all. After receiving your email, we will immediately delete your account as well as your personal information that we have
