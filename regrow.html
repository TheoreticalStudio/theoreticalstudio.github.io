About the game

Regrow is a fresh auto battler that is easy to learn but hard to master.

Collect colorful creatures, combine them to create the best team, and clash with other people for glory and legacy.

Every creature you find can grow into a bigger, better, and cuter one! Every single one of them is unique and has a different flavor; qualities both evident and not-so-obvious.

Play the game at your own pace, and be as competitive as you want. The game will suit both casual and hardcore players without compromising the experience for either of them.

Collect different creatures from melons, combine them to create the best team and play with other people. Every creature you find can be grown into bigger, better and cuter one!

  • Combine them into the most tasteful team
  • Grow your creatures to be juicier, cuter, and more powerful
