About the game

Regrow is a ripe and fresh auto battler that is easy to learn, but hard to master.

Collect colorful creatures, combine them to create the best team, and clash with other people for honor and glory.

Every creature you find can grow into a bigger, better, and cuter version! Every single one of them is unique and has a different flavor and qualities both evident and not-so-obvious.

Play the game at your own pace, and be as competitive as you want. The game will suit both casual and hardcore players without compromising the experience for either of them.

  • Collect the coolest creatures you can find
  • Combine them into the unbeatable team
  • Grow your creatures to be fierce, effective and cute :3
  • Climb the ladder by competing against other players for weekly rewards
