About the game

  • **Collect** the freshest creatures you can find
  • **Combine** them into the most tastefull team
  • **Grow** your creatures to be juicier, cuter and more powerful
  • **Harvest** success amongst other players in a friendly competition

    • **Regrow** is a fresh and easy-going auto battler. Collect different creatures from melons, combine them to create the best team and play with other people. Every creature you find can be grown into bigger, better and cuter one! All creatures have different flavours to them; qualities both obvious and not-so-obvious. Play the game at your own pace, be as competitive as you want to be. The game will suit both casual and more competitive players without compromising the experience for either of them
